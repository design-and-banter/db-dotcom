This month's event: D&B#1

Hello world

Join us for our first event: 7pm, Monday the 3rd of June
at Bloomsbury Lanes.

The format of the night is simple. There will be four talks, seven minutes each. What we are aiming for is two talks explaining a concept, idea or technique and two show’n’tells. Each talk will contain at least 2 minutes for questions and feedback.

Questions and critique are encouraged. Beers, bowling and banter will follow.

Our first event is kindly supported by our friends at Evently.
