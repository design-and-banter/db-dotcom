This month's event: D&B#1

Hello world

Join us for our first event: 7pm, Monday the 3rd of June
at Bloomsbury Lanes.

The format of the night is simple. There will be three talks, seven minutes each. What we are aiming for is a mixture of talks: some explaining a concept, idea or technique and some show’n’tells. Each talk will contain at least 2 minutes for questions and feedback.

Questions and critique are encouraged. Beers, bowling and banter will follow.

Our first event is kindly supported by our friends at Evently.
