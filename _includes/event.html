This month: D&B#9

“Designing 9 to 5”

7pm, Tuesday, February 4th at Shoreditch Village Hall

We're excited to be back at Shoreditch Village Hall for the ninth installment of Design+Banter. There are some great speakers lined up — one flying in all the way from Austin, Texas — as well as the usual locally-sourced craft beer and 175+ designers all in one room. This month the event is very kindly supported by IBM Design. Yes "that" IBM. If you love design, you're guaranteed to love this.
