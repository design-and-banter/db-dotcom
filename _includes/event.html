"10x Designer”

7pm, Monday, March 3rd at BL_NK

We're excited to be back at the wonderful BL-NK for the 10th installment of Design+Banter. As usual we have some excellent speakers lined up for you, the usual open bar and, of course, 175+ of London's best and brightest design talent, all under one roof. If you love design, you'll love this. Follow us on Twitter for speaker announcements.

This month's Design+Banter speakers

Say hello to Cole, Laura and Nicklas.

  • Cole Peters

    Cole is a designer, writer, noise-maker and obsessively curious craftsman. A Canadian in London, he’s currently working on launching @firstthings2014. Previously, he co-founded and designed at Burdocks.

    @cole_peters

  • Laura Kalbag

    Laura Kalbag is a designer easily excited by web design and development. A freelancer for the whole of her professional life, she revels in working with small and meaningful clients, creating websites, apps, icons, illustrations and the odd logo.

    @laurakalbag

  • Nicklas de León Persson

    Nicklas grew up in the north of Sweden. For the last decade he’s been teaching web design at the Art College in Belfast, published some books and spoken at conferences worldwide. Last year he moved to London to start up the @rumblelabs field office.

    @takete
