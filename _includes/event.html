The full run-down

The format of the night is simple. Everyone arrives at 7:00pm for free beers and to get to know each other before the talks start.

There are three talks of seven minutes each and we aim for a mixture of topics — some explaining a concept, some a technique or skill and some "war stories".

Each talk is then followed by a Q&A session with the speaker. Once the talks are finished the beers continue and the bowling gets going. At some point late in the night we all go home.
